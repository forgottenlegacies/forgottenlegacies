Discord Bot ("Soundboi 2000")

We have designed a Discord bot in order to play audio directly into a channel. Audio clips are selected via web interface and played through the bot. Users sign into the site using Discord Authentication. Currently, the interface is designed for "sondboard" type audio clips (WAV) and Youtube URL audio. We plan to make a more advanced player for song files. Download is not available yet, as we are continuing to make improvments.

Cryptographer

Firebelley's most popular game, Cryptographer, is a challenging puzzle-platformer complete with 50 levels to test your skills. Make use of bombs, switches, gates, and cannons to clear a path to the escape portal. This game can be played solo or with friends locally or through Steam Remote Play. Watch the promo video to get a better sense for the game and check it out on Steam!

Tenacious

Tenacious was Firebelley's first game project. It is a fast-paced rogue-lite dungeon crawler with a heavy emphasis on loot. Tear your way through levels and swap gear on the fly to change your abilities. Loot rare items to enhance your abilities. Spend your gold at the forge to create your own play style. This game will no longer be available on Steam once his newest game, Sword Slinger, Launches.
